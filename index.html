School Auditiorium

Established in 1991.

The auditorium is equipped with modern and state of the art audio visual facilities, that is the envy of many. The auditorium has a seating capacity of 700.

School Library

Established in 1991.

The Doon Blossom Academy Library System comprises of a Central Library and 1 departmental library that collectively support the teaching, research and extension programmes of the Institute.

The Doon Blossom Academy Yoga Ground is a ground which is made for doing activities like karate and yoga.
